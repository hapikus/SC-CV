RSS Statistics

 "Rss statistics" is a website that provides comprehensive analytics and data visualization for the JS FE 2023 Q1 course.

 The target audience includes both students and mentors of the course.

 The website offers three groups of reports focusing on students, tasks, and mentors, utilizing various data visualization techniques such as tables, pie charts, bar and line graphs, as well as funnels to present the course statistics effectively.
